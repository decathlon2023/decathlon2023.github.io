SIA Academic Decathlon 2023

How will it work?
This competition will assess the following subjects:
1. General Knowledge
2. Maths
3. Physics
4. Chemistry
5. Biology
6. Impromptu Speech Writing.
• Please see the "Schedule and Syllabus" page for mote details
• Please note that the topics listed under the syllabus are not an exhaustive

• Each round will be composed of 10-15 questions, if time permits, aimed at both year 12 and 13 students. The question difficulty will gradually increase and hence, so will the number of allocated points per question.
• For some of the questions, a timer will be set and each team who gets the question correct will attain the respective questions. However, for more complex questions, a time limit will also be set, but the first team to reach the correct answer will attain the points. Our host will explain the mark allocation on the day of the event
